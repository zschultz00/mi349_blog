About Zachary Schultz

Bio:

Zachary Schultz is an undergraduate student at Michigan State University (MSU). He enjoys learning about anything technology and or computer related. He, along with some of his peers, co-founded a Linux User Group at MSU, Spartan Linux Users Group (SLUG). Zachary is interested in researching augmented reality and virtual reality in relation to privacy and business. Zachary is an undergraduate research assistant within SPARTIE Lab.
